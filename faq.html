Frequently Asked Questions

Why did I become a Web Developer?

I thoroughly enjoy learning how things work, also how to fix things that are broken. For a long time I was interested in becoming a mechanical engineer. I got introduced into the hardware side of technology through a work opportunity and that is where I got introduced into the role of software. It fascinates me to have the chance to not only fix things, but to create something. And that is where the desire to become a Web Developer originated.

What is your average project turnaround?

As of now I am still learning. But my average completion time for my assignments is three hours.

What is your design process?
